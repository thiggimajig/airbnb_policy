Methodology and Data Notes

Airbnb does not provide any public data to aid cities or researchers in understanding the platform's impact
All data on Airbnb is from the batch of scraped Airbnb published on Inside Airbnb on January 2021
The Airbnb listings' precise geo location is obscured by Airbnb's privacy buffer, this is especially relevant when considering listings within the same building
All data on the city (ie. population, residents, renters, residential units, bedrooms) comes from the 2011 Census Data from Open Data Toscana
Since income data is considered private, Neighborhood Rent Burden data came from average salaries in Florence
Airbnb listings have been filtered to only show active listings which was defined by any listing who has had at least one review in the past 12 months
The style of this dashboard was created by Volt bootstrap templates unless otherwise specified.
